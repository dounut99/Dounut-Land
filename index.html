How to get a donut!

First, you get 4 cups of flours. You can get the cups from grinding flowers. Next, 2 large eggs from candy chickens, 1/4 cup sugar from the sugercane forest. After, 1 1/8 cups milk, 1/2 teaspoon salt from any rock candy caves. Following that 3 teaspoons yeast from the potato farms and 4 cups oil. Lastly, 1 1/4 cup butter. You can get that from the butterfly pollen.

The Mixing Process

Start with a bowl. Start with the milk, sugar, yeast and let it sit. Take butter and eggs in a bowl to mix. After 8 minutes of mixing add the two mixtures together. Put the dough in a oiled pan and into the fridge. After 8 hours leave it in the window house made for donuts.After it is made you can add all the sprinkles or DNA.
